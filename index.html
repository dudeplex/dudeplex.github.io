Tell Me More!
As you may have heard, Looney's Texan reign is coming to an end. Seven hot years and a lot of cold beers later, he's headed back to his back to the frigid motherland, the great midwest city of Detroit (pronounced Day-Twah).

So here's what that means for you. On Friday October 18th, you are going to leave work early. Like, really early. You're then going to drive over to the Plex, where we will already have drank more beer than you. Not to worry though, my friend, because what we'll lack in sobriety will be more than made up for with a bountiful supply of delicious taps, homemade right in our backyard. You'll bring a lawn chair if you have one, your swimsuit, a snack, and a thirst for adventure, because this party ain't stoppin' til about 8pm when we all pass out in the yard. Just kidding, this party ain't stoppin 'til we say so, and that's not any reasonable hour!
